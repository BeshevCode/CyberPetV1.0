HOW TO PLAY :

Press the buttons to fill your pet's needs.

If any of the bars gets empty, you won't be able to fill the bar again by pressing the relevant refill button,
unless you purchase the required item from the "Item Shop" that cures the relevant bar!

If you manage to keep all the bars up and collect enough cash, you can upgrade your pet to a Cowboy and then to a Rapper and COMPLETE THE GAME!

However if all three bars get to 0, it's GAME OVER!

Thanks for playing!
